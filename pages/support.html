Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Browse our shop, add books to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to create an account or login first.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit cards, debit cards, and online banking.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days. Express delivery is available for 1-2 business days.

Can I cancel my order?

Yes, you can cancel your order before it's processed. Go to your Orders page and click Cancel.
